Hello!

Thank you for trying out Open Data Press v0.1.

Open Data Press makes it easy to publish Open Data from your Google Spreadsheets.

  1. Create a spreadsheet in Google Drive.
    Share, collaborate and refine your data as usual.
  2. Design a custom template in OpenDataPress.
    Format your data the way you want it - any mark-up, any schema.
  3. Publish your Open Data.
    Your Open Data is automatically updated whenever your Google Spreadsheet is changed.

This first release is a bit rough around the edges, but hopefully clear enough to demonstrate the concept.

To find out more, check out the wiki.

Getting Started

Get started by Logging in.

If you're running your own instance, be sure to include your Google API keys in the configuration files, otherwise nothing will work. ;)

Reporting Problems
